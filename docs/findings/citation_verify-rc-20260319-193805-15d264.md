Standard quantization-aware training (QAT) and post-training quantization (PTQ) optimize float-space or fake-quantized objectives and interact with deployment codecs only at export time, leaving a gap between training objectives and the true leaderboard metric. This work introduces ZQAT, a codec-roundtrip QAT framework that embeds the int8+zlib deployment stack into a staged training schedule tailored to Parameter Golf lane 3.
